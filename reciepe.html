Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 8 slices

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¾ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and oil. Mix well.
  4. Pour batter into greased pans and bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Let the cake cool, then frost as desired.
